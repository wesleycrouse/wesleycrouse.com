I am a quantitative scientist with a PhD in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology. My research experience includes developing statistical approaches for genetic association with gene expression and other biomedical traits in model organisms. I have expertise in Bayesian nonparametric approaches, Bayesian model selection, and generalized linear models, with implementation in the R and Stan statistical computing languages.

I am interested in developing and applying cutting-edge methods to analyze large and complex datasets. I am currently seeking biomedical research opportunities involving sparsity, machine learning, casual inference, or other innovative statistical approaches.
